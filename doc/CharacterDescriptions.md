### Abia Nawazi
`[HP2]` | **Role:** Main dateable girl
**Summary:** Sweet, unassertive girl with a crippling sex addiction she can barely contain in public.
- **Personality:** Caring, understanding, unassertive, people-pleasing

### Aiko Yumi
`[HP1, HCS]` | **Role:** Main dateable girl
**Summary:** Unmotivated, easygoing physics professor who gambles and drinks to cope with her regrets.
- **Personality:** Laid back, open-minded, uninspired, non-judgmental
- **Speech:** Casual, relaxed; exclaims "Goodbye problems!" when offered a drink

### Jessie Maye
`[HP1, HP2, HCS]` | **Role:** Main dateable girl in both titles
**Summary:** Carefree adult actress and single mother in HP1; depressed and aging in HP2 as her career fades and estrangement from Tiffany deepens.

**HP1 version:**
- **Personality:** Carefree, flirtatious, nostalgic, wants to be pampered; prefers open relationships
- **Speech:** Relaxed and warm; expressive about her needs

**HP2 version:**
- **Personality:** Depressed, emotionally vulnerable, seeking acceptance; still warm but hollowed out
- **Speech:** More subdued; longing for romantic validation
- **Quirks (HP2):** Tiffany has blocked her on social media; fewer film offers due to age; worsened drinking and smoking habits; developed emphysema; preferred trait shifted from sexuality to romance
- **Relationships:** Mother of Tiffany (estranged in HP2); Abia is her biggest fan (alias: Misty Katz)
- **Occupation:** Adult film actress (alias: Misty Katz)
