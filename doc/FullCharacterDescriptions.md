## Character Descriptions

For reference these are descriptions of each character curtsey of the HuniePop wiki

### Abia Nawazi

debuted as one of the main dateable girls in HuniePop 2. She’s kind to others, but has an addiction to everything related to sex. She has trouble staying focused during conversation or at work, and is Jessie’s biggest fan, through her alias Misty Katz.

Abia gets along with the protagonist and the other girls, but she tends to speak with an unassertive attitude, often stuttering. Her main personality trait is her addiction to sex and porn. She is always thinking of sex, has trouble going through her day without masturbating multiple times, and misspeaks, usually saying something sexual instead of the word she meant, like when she introduced herself as Labia instead of Abia. Besides her addiction, she is very caring and understanding towards others, as she grew up in a strict household and had a lot of trouble getting accustomed to speaking English after coming to the island. She asks the protagonist to put the other girl’s needs above hers.

### Aiko Yumi 

is one of the main dateable girls in HuniePop, and was made into a recruitable girl in HunieCam Studio. She is a physics professor for the University of Glenberry and dislikes her job. She is friendly and easygoing, and uses gambling and drinking to cope with her issues.

Aiko is a very laid back character. She is a professor at the University and expresses regret for pursuing her master's degree in mathematics. She comes off as very unmotivated and uninspired, and enjoys gambling at the casino to make some money to pay off her debt. She also exclaims “Goodbye problems!” when offering her a drink, and is notably one of few girls who is willing to drink at any time of day. She doesn’t enjoy much physical activity, as she initially responds with sleeping as her hobby, enjoys the lazy river at the water park, and prefers to spend time at home on the couch for a vacation. Aiko is very open to any conversation topics, such as dating history, political views, or sexual interests.

While HunieCam Studio is not canon, Aiko is characterized slightly different by her trying to find something to do after recently getting fired for having sex with her students, so she picked up camming.

### Ashley Rosemarry 

is one of the main dateable girls introduced in HuniePop 2. She is nonserious and laid back, and makes music as her hobby. She is one of the girls the protagonist is dating before the events of the game, and happily agrees to have threesomes with the protagonist. Ashley is a guest character, as she is the OC of the lead artist, Kopianget.

Ashley has a playful attitude towards the protagonist, as she openly asks sexual or shock value questions for her own amusement. She gives the protagonist the pet name sweet cheeks, claiming it's a term of endearment. She’s very laid back and open minded to any gifts or questions the player has for her, and likes to crack jokes when beginning a date. Ashley reveals that she was previously engaged, but backed out of it. She says that she used to be a “good little girl”, but grew out of it, and she wouldn’t have discovered her band or modeling career if she did get married.

### Audrey Belrose 

is one of the main dateable girls in HuniePop, and is a recruitable girl in HunieCam Studio. She is crass in her interactions with other girls, and is skeptical of the protagonist at first, but slowly mellows out throughout her dialogue.

Audrey is loud and brash, both towards her friends and people she dislikes. She’s not afraid of expressing her emotions about any given thing, and tends to see others as beneath her. She is uninterested in university and working, so she uses her looks to get men to buy her things, and then insults them behind their backs for trying to court her. She enjoys getting high and partying at Lustie’s Nightclub, and is always willing to drink alcohol no matter the time of day. After introducing herself, she is much more laid back when talking to the protagonist, but has occasional outbursts, such as when asked what her favorite color is.

While HunieCam Studio is not canon, Audrey is described as recently developing resentment for men because her ex, the HuniePop protagonist, was cheating on her with multiple other women. She does cam shows to get money from male viewers and uses it to buy hard drugs. Her dialogue is slightly more aggressive in this title as well.

Audrey was a scrapped character for HuniePop 2 early in development to keep the cast at 12 girls, so not much is known about her new personality. From her new design, she seemed to have had a major character development, as she dresses modestly and has a cross around her neck. Huniedev didn’t elaborate when asked about this concept, but said that if he did continue to design her, he would have removed the cross and went in a different direction, as he does not want religion to be mentioned in his games.

### Beli Lapran 

is one of the main dateable girls in HuniePop, and one of the recruitable cam girls in HunieCam Studio. She is friendly and loves nature, and works as a yoga instructor at the gym.

Beli is down to earth and sincere in her dialogue. She has several hobbies, such as collecting items from nature like pinecones and shells, and does yoga instructing as her job. Beli puts a lot of thought into her words, and tries to see the most in each day; she is very appreciative of being alive and tries to motivate the protagonist and her friends like Aiko to be active with yoga to destress. She’s self conscious about her body, as she’s described as chubby compared to the other girls. Kyu makes a one off comment about Beli being a closet freak, but this isn’t showcased in any of her dialogue.

### Brooke Belrose 

made her debut in the series in HunieCam Studio as a recruitable camgirl, and then became one of the main dateable girls in HuniePop 2. She is the acting resort owner, as her husband is the actual owner, and is using the island as an opportunity to have some space from her family. She’s flirtatious, materialistic, and assertive.

Brooke is very straightforward towards the other girls and the protagonist, and about her personal needs. When first meeting her, she is quick to ask if the protagonist and Jessie are sexual partners, and opens up about her sugar baby arrangement with Sarah. Brooke is very materialistic and is attracted to suitors who have a lot of money, as seen whenever she speaks of her purchases or using her husband’s money for cosmetic surgery. Brooke also has a promiscuous personality, usually being the one to initiate the threesome scenes, and is open minded to any of the sex toys when offered.

While HunieCam is not canon, Brooke is depicted as a bored housewife looking to find young men online to expose herself to. Her lines reveal she wishes to keep her camgirl job a secret from her husband.

### Candace "Candy" Crush 

was introduced in HunieCam Studio as one of the recruitable girls, and became one of the main dateable girls in HuniePop 2. She is a cheerful girl, often thinking about cute animals or her work at the strip club, but tends to be absent minded when trying to remember things or sharing information.

Candace has a very bubbly personality, but lacks critical thinking skills. She often says the first thing that comes to mind, such as silly comments about the date locations. Her short and long term memory are lacking, as she doesn’t know basic counting, has speech grammar issues, and at times doesn’t have social awareness, such as when she gets loud when buying coke from Nora. It is unknown when these mental problems occurred, but it may be since she was young, as she admits to playing with baby toys when she was a teenager and possibly not graduating high school, leading to her getting her GED instead. Candace enjoys working as a stripper, but dislikes when her customers insult her or her intelligence.

### Celeste Luvendass 

is one of the secret girls found in HuniePop. She is an alien from the planet Tendricide. She’s stranded on Earth, and accepts the protagonist’s company. She’s very intelligent and easily flustered.

Celeste is very demanding when you first meet her because you have her ship part, but warms up to the protagonist after asking her to stay for a bit. She finds them endearing and gets flustered by their advances. She has a lot of knowledge of astronomy and math, and all of her questions are related to space terms and facts about planets in the Milky Way. She’s not accustomed to the way humans live or act, and finds their habits odd, such as celebrating birthdays and bra cup sizes.

### Jessie Maye 

is one of the dateable girls in HuniePop and HuniePop 2: Double Date, and is a recruitable girl in HunieCam Studio. She works as an adult actress by the alias Misty Katz, and spends her days off at the bar drinking.

In HuniePop, Jessie is carefree and flirtatious towards her friends and the protagonist. She loves her daughter Tiffany and shares her enjoyment of the holidays because of nostalgia. She’s had to make sacrifices to raise Tiffany, such as dropping out of high school, and developed a smoking addiction in her teens. While talking to Jessie, she expresses that she’d like to be pampered and taken care of by her partner, and prefers an open relationship.

While not canon to the HuniePop games, Jessie’s HunieCam description says she is looking for new opportunities because she is getting less film offers in the industry. This detail may have been reused for Jessie’s HuniePop 2 personality.

In HuniePop 2, Jessie is described as “Depressed” in her initial reveal, and throughout the game, it is revealed that Tiffany blocked her on social media and she is receiving less offers in the adult film industry because she’s getting older. Jessie’s preferred trait is now romance instead of sexuality, and this is emphasized in dialogue as she wants acceptance from a romantic partner despite her age. Due to her depression and inability to break habits, her drinking and smoking habits have become more frequent, leading to her developing emphysema.

### Jewn 

is one part of The Nymphojinn, with Moxie as her other half, being a pair of Cosmic Deities that wreak destruction upon the galaxy unless they have sex with a human every 10,000 years. The pair are the final bosses of HuniePop 2, and are the last threesome needed to complete the game.

Jewn is hostile towards Kyu and the protagonist after being awoken from her 10,000 year slumber. She teases the protagonist and has more of a tendency to belittle them for their mistakes, and claims no one is worthy of them. After 4 successful date rounds, Jewn gives in and proceeds to have a threesome with the protagonist and Moxie.

### Kyanna Delrio 

is one of the main dateable girls in HuniePop, and one of the first recruitable girls in HunieCam Studio. She enjoys working out at the local gym in her spare time, as she works as a hairdresser and has a child to take care of.

Kyanna is upbeat and friendly throughout HuniePop. She’s the one who initially approaches the protagonist and introduces herself. She says she was previously a party girl but after having her son, is looking to have a more stable life, both for herself and for potential partners. She is very into fitness, as she works out every single day and is familiar with most regulars at the gym. Kyanna is not afraid to express her emotions, as a lot of her dialogue is yelling in excitement or during her argument with Audrey, cursing in her face.

### Kyu Sugardust 

is the first girl introduced in the games, and is a love fairy from Sky Garden, and is the mascot of the HuniePop series. She acts as the tutorial and guide for each game, and is a secret dateable girl in HuniePop. Kyu’s a bit bossy in a playful way, and tries her best to help the protagonist.

Kyu acts as the witty and smug sidekick in each of the games. She’s the first girl the protagonist meets, and makes them her client to help them meet and date attractive women around town. She gives advice and commentary about each of the girls, usually finding them attractive herself. Kyu’s personality is a mix of fraternity bro and a cutesy girl, as she plays both a wingman rooting for the protagonist and a pink magical fairy.

Kyu’s personality in HuniePop 2 is more intense, as she’s still the protagonist's guide but a lot of her dialogue revolves around sex. She’s more assertive than she previously was, and is more judgmental towards the protagonist and the girls.

### Lailani Kealoha 

was introduced in HunieCam Studio as a recruitable cam girl, and later became one of the main dateable girls in HuniePop 2. She is a native of Inna De Poona and works as the clerk at the Love Lei Hotel. She is friendly and wants to assure everyone of a nice time wherever she goes.

Lailani is kind towards everyone she interacts with, but is very timid by nature. Whenever she speaks of one of her hobbies, Lailani becomes very enthusiastic, such as yoga, massage therapy, and lucid dreaming. Lailani is very insecure about the way that she looks, disliking her eye shape and nose, or wishing her boobs and butt were bigger, to the point that when asking what part of her body she likes the most, she replies she doesn’t think there’s anything special about her body. Potentially due to a long distance relationship that ended with her ex cheating on her, Lailani shares in some dialogue that she wishes for a partner that accepts her for her flaws. Lailani is also inexperienced with sex, leading to her feeling awkward whenever it’s brought up, and wasn’t aware that Inna de Poona was an island for people looking for orgies to vacation to.

### Lillian Aurawell 

makes her debut in HunieCam Studio as a recruitable girl, and was then made into a main dateable girl in HuniePop 2. She’s defiant to her parents, dropping out of high school and having sex to rebel against them. She is a goth girl archetype and jokes about death, usually her own, in her dialogue.

Lillian has an edgy personality. She dislikes activity based dates and would rather spend her time sleeping or using her phone. A lot of her dialogue revolves around the idea of her dying in various ways, such as drowning or falling off a tall building. She is revulsed by any romantic gestures, and she’s more interested in partying or having sex.

### Lola Rembrite 

is one of the main dateable girls in HuniePop and HuniePop 2: Double Date, and is a recruitable cam girl in HunieCam Studio. She is a flight stewardess for Tropica Airlines and is preparing to launch a fashion brand. She is characterized as a confident and independent girl who is always working towards her goals.

Lola is self assured and confident. She likes to motivate herself towards completing her goals, and has a prideful attitude about the life she’s building. She enjoys physical activity, such as sports, and mentions her love of adventure and traveling the world through her job.

While HunieCam Studio is not canon, Lola’s attitude towards her job changes, as she begins to dislike passengers and begins doing cam shows instead.

In HuniePop 2, Lola’s personality has slightly evolved to make her more of a workaholic. She’s now a fashion designer and is now focused on building her brand, beginning work on designing a fashion line, and looking for inspiration. While it’s not apparent, Lola is a lot more stressed, and this has resulted in her relying on caffeine to get her through her days, and becoming a lot more sexually open, as she’s more flirtatious towards the protagonist and the girls she pairs with. Lola also has an attitude of “work hard now to secure a better future”, as dialogue suggests she’s willing to deal with low points in her life to get to the high points.

### Marlena

Marlena is a bit self centered, shown in dialogue telling the protagonist that the pleasure is all theirs. She’s interested in suitors who can provide for her, and is said to have gone through life because of her good looks. She has a calm demeanor and is confident in whatever you ask her to do.

### Momo 

is one of the secret girls in HuniePop. She is a cat girl who you meet when you throw out a pet fish outdoors. She has a bubbly personality and enjoys quizzing the protagonist on cat facts.

Momo is energetic and naive, as she represents a young cat. She enjoys running around, takes care of the fish you give her, and tends to sleep a lot throughout the day. Little is known about her upbringing or what she did before meeting the protagonist, but while she has issues with grammar and basic learning, she is very knowledgeable about cats.

### Moxie

### Nikki Ann-Marie 

is one of the main dateable girls in HuniePop, and is one of the first recruitable girls in HunieCam Studio. She is a reserved girl and has an interest in retro video games.

Nikki is an introvert in HuniePop, and is the shy, nerdy girl of the cast. When meeting her for the first time, she see through the protagonist's attempt to charm her, and leaves. After a bit, she comes back and apologizes, and introduces herself. Nikki’s dialogue tends to be pessimistic, and she has very little hopes for the protagonist. She reveals that nobody has treated her so nicely before, and has moments where she warms up in dialogue.

While HunieCam is not canon, the game characterizes Nikki as becoming a video game streamer who then started doing cam shows instead of working at her Cafe job. This version of her seems to have inspired the scrapped Nikki design for HuniePop 2, where she becomes a stereotypical female Twitch streamer. Not much more is known about HuniePop 2 Nikki, as she didn’t make it far into development before HunieDev decided to keep the cast at 12 girls.

### Nora Delrio 

originated from HunieCam Studio as a recruitable girl, and later became one of the dateable girls in HuniePop 2. She’s a hotel maid for Love Lei Hotel and other locations around the resort. She is cousins with Kyanna, as the two share a last name and mentions Kyanna a few times in game. She’s described as being tomboyish or tough, but she is very casual and friendly towards the protagonist.

Nora is normally laid back and goes with the flow during conversation. She’s been to prison and presents herself with a tough exterior, as she expresses that life is tough and she accepts the cards dealt to her, and tries to overcome her hardships, even if that means she has to sell drugs or steal valuables for money. She’s not afraid to speak up for herself if someone is bothering her, and reveals in her vindictive baggage that after another girl in prison “couldn’t keep her mouth shut”, she trashed her place and had sex with her partner when she got out to get back at her. Despite romance being her favorite trait, she advises the protagonist that what they have is strictly casual, making her very emotionally reserved. She loves her big family and wishes to have kids for herself one day.

### Tiffany Maye 

is one of the main dateable girls in HuniePop, and is one of the first recruitable girls in HunieCam Studio. She’s a studious college student who enjoys cheerleading and spending time with her friends. Tiffany is very friendly towards the protagonist in her dialogue. Kyu describes her as a “girl next door” type. While HunieCam Studio is not canon to the main series, Tiffany is one of the first cam girls the player can recruit. She’s characterized as doing cam shows to make money to pay through college, but maintains her cheery disposition in voice clips.

### Zoey Greene 

debuted in HunieCam Studio as a recruitable girl, and was chosen to be one of the main dateable girls in HuniePop 2. She’s an oddball character who can see the Love Fairies, and helps the protagonist get fairy wings by bonding with girls.

Zoey is an eccentric character. When first meeting her, she reveals that she is aware of the Love Fairies existence, and the impending destruction from the Nymphojinn, and actively seeked out the protagonist to help. Throughout the game, Zoey reveals that she has psychic abilities, such as premonitions through dreams and receiving “readings” from one of the other 11 girls on the island. She finds concepts such as emailing and digital photos amazing, and doesn’t understand why clothes are important to wear in society. Zoey also has elements to her personality that are similar to a robot, such as needing to stay in a cooler climate, her comfort in the computer lab in high school, and even her admitting she does not feel completely human.

In HunieCam, Zoey was portrayed as being an “insufferable millennial” who identified as an android. HunieCam is not canon, but this personality was then translated to her previously unused HuniePop 2 persona, where she was a far left advocate who disliked capitalism and did things because she wanted to. HunieDev felt like this characterization was "too cringey to write for a full game", and decided that he would not include any political dialogue in any HuniePop game.
